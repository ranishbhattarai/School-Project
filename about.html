About us

Trinity International College hosts an annual SciTech program where students from our college and other colleges come together to showcase their innovative projects. This event provides a platform for young minds to present their ideas, collaborate with peers, and gain valuable feedback from industry experts. Each year, the SciTech program highlights the creativity and technical skills of students, fostering a spirit of innovation and excellence. The event also includes workshops, guest lectures, and panel discussions on the latest trends in science and technology. Participants have the opportunity to network with professionals, explore career opportunities, and enhance their knowledge in various fields. We believe that the SciTech program not only celebrates the achievements of our students but also inspires them to pursue their passions and contribute to the advancement of science and technology.
